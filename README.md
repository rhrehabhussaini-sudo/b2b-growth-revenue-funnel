# B2B Growth & Revenue Funnel Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on designing and analyzing a complete B2B growth and revenue funnel using data analytics and CRM-style logic. The goal is to understand how leads move through the funnel, identify drop-off points, and highlight opportunities for revenue growth.

The project simulates how marketing, sales, and revenue teams use data to track performance and improve conversion efficiency.

---

## Business Problem
B2B companies often generate leads but struggle to:
- Convert leads into qualified opportunities
- Align marketing and sales performance
- Forecast revenue accurately

This project addresses these challenges by structuring a clear, data-driven funnel.

---

## Funnel Stages Covered
- Leads Generated
- Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs)
- Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs)
- Opportunities
- Closed-Won Deals

Each stage is analyzed to understand conversion rates and revenue impact.

---

## Key Metrics & KPIs
- Lead-to-MQL Conversion Rate
- MQL-to-SQL Conversion Rate
- Opportunity Win Rate
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Revenue Contribution by Funnel Stage

---

## Tools & Skills Demonstrated
- Python (Pandas, basic analytics)
- Funnel analysis & KPI calculation
- CRM-style thinking (Sales & Marketing alignment)
- Business and revenue insights
- GitHub documentation & project structure

---

## Key Insights
- Conversion drop-offs highlight bottlenecks in the funnel
- Improving early-stage lead quality has a strong impact on revenue
- Sales-qualified leads contribute disproportionately to total revenue

---

## Outcome
This project demonstrates how analytics can be applied to real-world B2B growth and revenue challenges. It reflects a practical understanding of how marketing and sales teams use data to optimize performance and drive growth.
